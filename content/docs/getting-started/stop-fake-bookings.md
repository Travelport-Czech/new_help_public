# Stop fake bookings

<!-- tags: fake booking, spam, blocked emails, abuse, fraud, code list -->

GOL IBE lets you block specific email addresses from placing bookings on your front-end. Use this whenever you identify an address that is sending fake or spam reservations.

## Steps

1. Log into the admin console.
2. Go to **Code Lists → Blocked e-mails**.
3. Click **Add**.
4. Enter the email address you want to block.
5. Click **Save**.

The address is blocked immediately — any booking attempt from that email will be rejected.

## Tips

- Block individual addresses as you spot abuse patterns.
- For broader protection, also review the **Handle bookings with clients** workflow, which ensures clients receive only a basic notification until you verify their identity — limiting what an abuser can do even if they complete the form.
