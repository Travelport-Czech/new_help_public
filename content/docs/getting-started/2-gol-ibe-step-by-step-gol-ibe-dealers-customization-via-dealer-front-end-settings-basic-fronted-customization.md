# Basic Front-end Customisation

<!-- tags: front-end, customisation, design, dealer, logo, colours, branding -->

Each dealer can have a uniquely branded front-end. You can customise the title, logo, colours, and other visual elements without writing any code.

## How to access front-end settings

1. Log into the GOL IBE admin console.
2. Go to **Dealers** and select the dealer you want to customise.
3. Click **Front-end settings**.

## What you can customise

### Title
Enter the text you want to appear as your front-end title.

### SEO
Add keywords and description text for search engine optimisation.

### Logo
Find the **Banner** section, tick the type, press **Change** and upload your logo from your computer. Specify the height in pixels and save.

### URL Homepage
Insert your agency website URL here. This link is placed under your logo — clicking the logo redirects visitors to your agency homepage.

### Colours
Colour customisation starts in the **Background** section. Click any coloured box and specify the colour you wish. Save changes and check the result on your front-end.

### Favicon
Find the **Favicon** section, upload your favicon from your computer, and save.

## Apply changes

After saving your settings, click **Flush HTML caches** (under Code Lists) to ensure changes appear immediately for visitors.
