# How to modify dates of departure and arrival on my GOL IBE search form

<!-- tags: departure date, calendar, search form, dealer edit -->

You can control from which date customers can search for flights on the GOL IBE booking engine.

## How to set up

1. Log into the GOL IBE admin console.
2. Go to **Dealers → Dealers** and open your dealer record.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Scroll down to the **Flight search** section.
5. Find the setting **Enable search from** and choose one of the options:
   - **Actual day** — customers can search for flights departing today
   - **A day before** — flights offered from tomorrow
   - **2 days before** — flights offered from 2 days from today
   - **3 days before** — flights offered from 3 days from today
6. Click **Save**.
