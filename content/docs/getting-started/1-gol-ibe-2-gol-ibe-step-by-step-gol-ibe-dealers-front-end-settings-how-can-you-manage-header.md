# How can you manage the header on your GOL IBE web?

<!-- tags: header, navigation, menu, front-end, branding, dealer, iframe -->

In case you would like to change the header of your GOL IBE web, feel free to choose the option **Iframe** in section **Front-end settings**.

- Log into admin console
- Go to section **Dealers - Front-end settings**
- Find section **Banner - Iframe**
- Insert the URL prepared by your company
- Save settings
