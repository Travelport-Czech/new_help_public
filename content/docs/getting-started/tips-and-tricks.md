## Colour palette tools

Helpful websites for designing and customising your GOL IBE front-end colours:

- [Coolors](https://coolors.co) — colour scheme generator
- [Adobe Color](https://color.adobe.com) — create colour palettes from images or rules
- [Paletton](https://paletton.com) — classic wheel-based colour designer
- [Colorsinspo](https://colorsinspo.com) — curated palettes and gradients
- [Color Hunt](https://colorhunt.co) — trending colour palettes
