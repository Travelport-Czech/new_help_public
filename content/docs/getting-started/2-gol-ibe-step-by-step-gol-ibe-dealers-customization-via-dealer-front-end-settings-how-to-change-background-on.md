# How to change background on your front-end?

<!-- tags: background, colour, front-end, design, dealer, customisation -->

You can change the colours of your GOL IBE booking engine front-end to match your brand.

## Steps

1. Log into the GOL IBE admin console.
2. Go to **Dealers** and select the dealer whose front-end you want to update.
3. Click **Front-end settings**.
4. Find the **Background** section.
5. Click any coloured box and specify the colour you wish.
6. Save changes.
7. Go to **Code Lists → Flush HTML Caches** and check the result on your GOL IBE front-end.
